Team

Krishna Jha

Krishna is a first generation entrepreneur. In 1999 he quit his software job to co-found ITFinity, one of the first wireless data companies globally. The company was backed by marquee angel investors, including the heads of McKinsey, Chryscap, Edelweiss etc.

ITFinity went on to create significant innovations in mobile software and consumer services space, with a customer list comprising of top telcos and phone manufacturers. The company was highly profitable and ranked among Deloitte Fast 500 tech companies for three years in running.

In early 2007 ITFinity was acquired by OnMobile, the telecom spin-off from Infosys, providing super normal returns to its investors.
